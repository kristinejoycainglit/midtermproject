BADOC BLISS

Blissful Badoc Delight

Badoc Bliss is a town in the Philippines, but it doesn't have a specific signature dish that is widely known. However, being part of the Ilocos Region, which is renowned for its delicious cuisine, there are several dishes that you can find in Badoc and its neighboring towns that are worth trying.

Kilawin kambing is a Badoc dish made from goat meat and skin that is cooked and marinated in vinegar and spices. It is a popular appetizer dish in the Philippines that is perfect with a bottle of beer.

Kilawen is a popular Ilocano dish that is made from raw meat or fish, marinated in vinegar, and seasoned with salt, pepper, and spices. It is usually served as an appetizer or a main course. There are many variations of kilawen, depending on the type of meat or fish used.

Pork Kilawin or Kilawing baboy is a Filipino dish that is typically made with grilled pork belly. The meat is cooked first before being prepared this way because it does not get cooked completely when soaked in vinegar.

Pork Kilawin

Ingredients:

2 lbs. grilled pork belly

1 cup cane vinegar

1 medium cucumber, diced

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon granulated white sugar

Red chilies

1 medium yellow onion, minced

1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

 Instructions:

Chop the pork belly into serving pieces. Set aside.

Meanwhile, combine vinegar, salt, ground black pepper, chili, and sugar. Mix well.

Arrange the pork, cucumber, and onion in a large bowl. Add the vinegar mixture. Stir.

Let it stay for 30 minutes to 1 hour.

Transfer to a serving plate. Serve.

Kilawing Kambing

Ingredients:

1/2 kilogram goat meat (with skin, get from thigh, neck, and shoulder)

Water (for boiling)

2 pcs Laurel Leaves

2 Red Onion (minced)

1 1/2 tablespoons Ginger (minced)

3 pcs Siling Haba (or siling labuyo, chopped)

1/2 cup Palm Vinegar (or coconut)

Salt and Pepper

 Instructions:

Boil the goat meat in water with laurel leaves until tender.

Remove the meat from the pot and let it cool down.

Cut the meat into small pieces.

In a bowl, mix the minced red onion, ginger, siling haba, vinegar, salt, and pepper.

Add the goat meat to the mixture and mix well.

Cover the bowl with cling wrap and refrigerate for at least an hour before serving.

Kilawin Fish

Ingredients:

1 lb Tanigue fillet, cubed

1 cup calamansi juice

1/2 cup vinegar

1/2 cup ginger, minced

1/2 cup onion, minced

1/2 cup chili pepper, minced

Salt and pepper to taste

 Instructions:

In a mixing bowl, combine the cubed Tanigue fillet with calamansi juice and vinegar.

Add minced ginger, onion, chili pepper, salt and pepper to the mixture.

Mix all ingredients well.

Cover the bowl with cling wrap and refrigerate for at least an hour.

Serve chilled
